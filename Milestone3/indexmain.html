Kai Natseway

Performer | Arranger | Teacher

Bio

Kai Natseway is a Junior at Northern Arizona University majoring in music with an emphasis in percussion performance. A graduate of Sandia High School (Albuquerque, NM), Kai studied with percussion instructor Jorge Gonzalez. At Sandia, Kai served as section leader of the marching band drumline and performed in the wind ensemble and jazz ensemble for multiple years. At NAU, Kai has been the principal percussionist of the Wind Symphony, Symphonic Band and Symphony Orchestra. In addition, Kai performed with the NAU Percussion Ensemble, the Panorama Steel Band (playing lead pan), and Jazz Combo III.

While at NAU, Kai has been a member of the NAU Percussion Society and Phi Mu Alpha, a music Fraternity. During the summer in 2018, he held a part-time percussion/visual instructor position at Del Norte High School (Albuquerque) teaching music to the percussion section and marching techniques to the entire Phantom Marching Band. Also, during that summer he served on the counseling staff of the NAU Curry Summer Music Camp.

Kai was a member of the 2019 Academy Drum and Bugle Corps (Tempe, AZ) where he played bass drum in a DCI (Drum Corps International) season in competition. There he worked primarily with corps instructors Noah Cole and Cisco Hance. His drum corps experience started when he marched Arsenal Drum and Bugle Corps (Albuquerque, NM) during the summer of 2017. He was also a member of the 2017 and 2018 New Mexico Adrena-line (Los Lunas, NM) where he played tenors in WGI (Winter Guard International) “Independent class A”
